Resistors

Carbon Film Resistor

A carbon film resistor is a type of resistor that uses carbon as the resistor material. It has a low cost and a good temperature stability.

Metal Film Resistor

A metal film resistor is a type of resistor that uses a thin metal film as the resistor material. It has a high precision and a low temperature coefficient.

Wirewound Resistor

A wirewound resistor is a type of resistor that uses a coil of wire as the resistor material. It has a high power rating and a low inductance.

Capacitors

Ceramic Capacitor

A ceramic capacitor is a type of capacitor that uses a ceramic material as the dielectric. It has a small size and a high frequency response.

Tantalum Capacitor

A tantalum capacitor is a type of capacitor that uses tantalum as the anode material and a solid or liquid electrolyte as the cathode material. It has a high capacitance density and a low equivalent series resistance.

Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor

An aluminum electrolytic capacitor is a type of capacitor that uses an aluminum oxide layer as the dielectric and an aluminum foil as the electrode. It has a high capacitance and a long lifespan.
